Unicycle Boston

Unicycle Boston is a unicycling club formed in August 2024 based in the Boston, Massachusetts area. We currently hold weekly meetups at Pemberton Basketball Courts near Porter Square, Cambridge.

Meetup Information

Unicycle Boston meetups are held at Pemberton Basketball Courts near Porter Square in Cambridge, MA. All skill levels of unicycling from beginners to experts are welcome to come!

We will have basketballs and court space to play Unicycle Basketball. Unicycle basketball is a competitive sport and has competitions at the national and international level. The basketball court and adjacent fencing are also suitable for beginners looking to ride, and for those looking to practice flatland tricks.

For those taking public transit, Pemberton Courts is closest to the Porter Square stop on the Red Line. There is street parking available adjacent to the basketball courts. Toilets are available in a portable restroom next to the baseball field.

Note: Due to the cold temperatures/winter weather, meetups are cancelled for the rest of the season and will resume in the spring.

We are holding a winter meetup on January 18, 2025 from 2-4 PM at The Mill Works. If you are attending, please plan to meet at the reception desk 10 - 15 minutes before 2:00. See the photo of the reception desk here: MillWorks1.jpg.
