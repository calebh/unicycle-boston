Unicycle Boston

Unicycle Boston is a unicycling club formed in August 2024 based in the Boston, Massachusetts area. We currently hold weekly meetups, alternating between Lincoln Park Skatepark near Union Square, Somerville and Pemberton Basketball Courts near Porter Square, Cambridge.

As we are a new group, we are currently evaluating meetup locations for suitability. Based on member feedback at meetups, we will determine if the current locations will remain permanent. Please double check this website and/or the Meetup.com schedule to ensure that you go to the correct location.

General Meetups

Our general meetups are held at Lincoln Park Skatepark in Somerville every other Saturday from 1:30 PM - 3:30 PM. If you are new to unicycling or are looking to learn, this is the meetup to come to! The skatepark features a variety of obstacles suitable for urban riding. Adjacent to the skatepark is a basketball court suitable for practicing unicycle basketball and flatland.

For those taking public transit, Lincoln Park is closest to the Union Square Green Line station. From the Red Line, the park is about half an hour walk from both Harvard Square and Central Square. By bus, you can take the 86 bus to Washington St @ Parker St. There is street parking available near the park.

Unicycle Basketball

As the name suggests, unicycle basketball is similar to regular basketball, except that all players ride unicycles! Unicycle basketball is a competitive sport and has competitions at the national and international level. The unicycle basketball meetup occurs at Pemberton Basketball Courts near Porter Square in Cambridge every other Thursday from 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM.

For those taking public transit, Pemberton Courts is closest to the Porter Square stop on the Red Line. There is street parking available adjacent to the basketball courts.
