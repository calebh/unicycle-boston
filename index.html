Unicycle Boston

Unicycle Boston is a unicycling club formed in August 2024 based in the Boston, Massachusetts area. We currently hold weekly meetups at various Boston locations. All skill levels of unicycling from beginners to experts are welcome to come!

Meetup Information

Unicycle Boston meetups are generally held at one of the following locations. Check the calendar below for full details for specific dates!

Pemberton Basketball Courts

This location is near Porter Square in Cambridge, MA. This outdoor area consists of three basketball courts surrounded by a chain link fence. This location is close to the Red Line and generally has plentiful street parking. Toilets are available in a portable restroom adjacent to the baseball field.

Lincoln Park

This location is near Union Square in Somerville, MA. Lincoln has one full sized basketball court as well as a skatepark. If you're feeling adventerous the skatepark could be used to practice street unicycling. The parking situation is generally a bit worse than Pemberton. If you need parking, check either the tiny lot on the north side of the park next to the Albert F. Argenziano School or one of the adjacent streets. Toilets are available in the portable restrooms adjacent to the skatepark.

MIT Lobby 10

This location is in MIT Building 10, directly underneath the dome. Generally these meetups are co-located with the MIT juggling group, held on Sundays from 3:00 - 6:00. If Lobby 10 is occupied, Lobby 13 may be used as a backup space.
